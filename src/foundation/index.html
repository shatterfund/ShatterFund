Shatter Foundation

Our Mission

The Shatter Foundation connects girls in underserved communities with access to capital, networks and markets to foster an inclusive economy and equitable entrepreneurship

Girls of color, especially African American and Hispanic girls, both bear the burden of race and gender disparity in business. We want to change this disparity into a network of #girlswhoshatter these barriers.

Shatter Foundation

What

The Shatter Foundation provides the first step on the entrepreneurial journey of young girls in underserved communities by providing education around the business side, micro-finance, and applying our niched expertise of investing in the important emerging market of female entrepreneurs.

Why

The Shatter Foundation, like the Shatter Fund, is rooted in the belief that women and girls are the pathway to economic success for the United States and the economies around the world. But, there are entire communities of entrepreneurial talent here in the US being overlooked due to lack of exposure to what is possible, and lack of access to the entrepreneurial ecosystem for which our country is known.

At Shatter Foundation we believe that people are not born entrepreneurs. But, where they are born is the key to their opportunity, or lack of. That's why our Foundation is 100% focused on those underserved communities where young girls just need a chance. The chance to think, create and innovate.

How

We will provide aspiring entrepreneurs with the foundational skills to start and scale businesses through our investing expertise, and by connecting them to our network of mentors, advisors and investors. Through the foundation we will allot monies for investing in minority underserved women who would otherwise have a hard time getting a loan or access to capital.

Shatter Foundation

Call to action, cash investment + mentorship

Our expertise of investing in women combined with our deep knowledge of the female economies and partnerships across the business and Tech ecosystems enables us to provide monetary grants and mentorship to aspiring young entrepreneurs.
